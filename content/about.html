Overview

We all know that R is a critical part of statistics these days, but it can have a steep learning curve and be intimidating to get started with. The Department of Statistical Sciences (DoSS) toolkit is a free series of open source online modules written by undergraduates, that their fellow students and the public can use to learn the essentials of R.

How to contribute

The best way to contribute fixes and minor typos is to make a pull request on GitHub. Please make the pull request to the functions folder rather than the specific component that you are using.

If you are interested in contributing lessons or modules, then please contact Rohan Alexander. We are particularly interested in partnering with an institution where the language of instruction is French to develop a French language version.

Please contact Rohan (rohan.alexander@utoronto.ca) with any questions, comments, and suggestions.
